About Me

Welcome to my page! I am a geoinformatics specialist with a background in geography, passionate about sustainability, climate change, urban planning, and green, innovative solutions to real-world problems. Currently, I am studying in the Copernicus Master in Digital Earth Erasmus Mundus Joint Programme at the University of Salzburg - EO & GI and Palacký University Olomouc - GeoVisualization & GeoCommunication.

In my free time, I enjoy hiking and photographing nature, as well as creating digital illustrations and drawings, playing video games. I often look for ways to combine these creative pursuits with my professional interests — and vice versa.
