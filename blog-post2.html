Sinergise Summer School 2023 - Ljubljana

In the summer of 2023, I had the opportunity to participate in the Summer School organized by Sinergise in the city of Ljubljana. The one-week workshop lasted from July 10 to 14. I got to know a lot of great professional people from neighboring countries and from further afield. Every day of the week, we took some kind of practical application, and Sinergise employees willingly presented its background processes in the EO Browser. At the end of the course, we developed a web application that displays Sentinel-2 images suitable for displaying and analyzing forest fires using JupiterNotebooks.
